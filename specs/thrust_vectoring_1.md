# Thrust Vectoring System Prototype

- your job is to generate a 3D CAD model of a system to be 3d printed that will house a rocket motor and vector its thrust

## Requirements
- their should be **3** parts total
    1. Inner Engine Housing
        - holds the rocket
        - can rotate on one axis
        - attaches to component 2
    2. Middle Housing
        - houses component 1
        - can rotate on one axis
        - attaches to component 3
    3. Outer Housing
        - attaches to rocket fuesalage

- the components should contain integrated pins/axels that fit into holes on their outer component housing it order to allow rotation
- this means the components will most likely have to be printed in halves, and assembled / fitted together post print
